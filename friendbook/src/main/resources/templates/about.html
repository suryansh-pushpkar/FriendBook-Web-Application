What is FriendBook?

A modern social experience built with Spring Boot and Java.

Secure Signup & Login

Users can create an account and log in securely using Spring Security with password encryption. We prioritize your data safety using BCrypt hashing and reCaptcha v3 protection.

User Profile

Update your profile picture, manage your favorite songs, books, and places. View your personal post gallery and track your follower/following counts in real-time.

Follow & Network

Connect with others across the organization. Send friend requests, accept followers, and grow your network. Our notification system keeps you updated on every new connection.

📸

Post Sharing

Share images with your followers. Full CRUD support for your own posts.

❤️

Interactive Feed

Like, unlike, and comment on posts to stay engaged with your circle.

🔔

Live Notifications

Get notified when someone follows you or sends a request.
